Our History

From a small preparatory school to a globally recognized academy, our journey is defined by a commitment to quality and growth.

1985: Founding

Greenwood Academy is established by Dr. Prashant Singh Rathore as a small, non-denominational preparatory school with a focus on classical education.

1998: Campus Expansion

The new Science & Technology wing opens, marking a strategic shift toward a comprehensive STEM curriculum.

2010: International Program

Launch of the Global Studies Program, including the first international exchange partnerships in Europe and Asia.

2020: Digital Transformation

The 'Greenwood 2.0' initiative introduces one-to-one devices for all students and a fully digitized learning management system.
