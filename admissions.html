The 4-Step Admissions Process

Our holistic approach ensures we find the best fit for both the student and our academy.

01

Inquiry & Visit

Begin by filling out our online inquiry form and scheduling a personal campus tour or attending an open house event.

02

Application Submission

Complete the full online application, including academic records, recommendations, and the student questionnaire.

03

Testing & Interview

Applicants will be invited for a standardized entrance test and a personal interview with a member of the Admissions Committee.

04

Decision & Enrollment

Successful applicants receive an acceptance letter and must submit their enrollment contract and deposit by the final deadline.

Key Application Timeline (2025-2026)

Please adhere to these important dates to ensure your application is reviewed promptly.

September 1st, 2025

Online Application Opens

All forms, including financial aid requests, become available.

October 15th, 2025

First Open House Event

Join us to meet faculty, speak with current students, and tour our facilities.

January 15th, 2026

Application & Financial Aid Deadline

All application materials, test scores, and financial aid documents must be submitted.

March 10th, 2026

Admissions Decisions Mailed

All candidates will be notified of the committee's decision via mail and portal update.

April 10th, 2026

Enrollment Contract & Deposit Due

Final deadline for accepting the offer of admission and securing your student's place.

Tuition & Financial Aid

Greenwood Academy is committed to making our exceptional education accessible to talented students regardless of their economic background.

2025-2026 Tuition Rate

The all-inclusive tuition for the upcoming academic year is $32,500. This fee covers all academic courses, mandatory fees, and most extra-curricular activities.

Financial Aid

We offer need-based financial assistance. Approximately 25% of our students receive aid, with the average grant covering 60% of tuition. Aid applications must be submitted by the January 15th deadline.
